In this task, you will see short texts in two groups. Each text is different, but also the two groups of text differ in general ways, e.g. about content or text style.

Your task is to identify these general differences and describe them concisely in the text box.

In the example below, the texts from Group 1 play all during the day, while the texts in Group 2 mostly play during the night.

Note:

  • All texts are different, but we are only interested in general differences between the two groups of texts, not between individual pairs of text.
  • The difference does not need to occur in every text. E.g. in group 2, the second text does not specify that it is night. But still, the main distinction between texts in group 1 and 2 is day versus night.
  • There can be zero, one or multiple general differences between the groups.
  • You will be asked to do this task multiple times with different texts. Afterwards, you will be asked about your experience and background.
